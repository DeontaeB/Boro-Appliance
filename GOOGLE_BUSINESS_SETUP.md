## Step 6: Get Reviews (CRITICAL FOR RANKING)

Reviews are one of the TOP 3 ranking factors for local SEO.

### Strategy:
1. **Get your first 10 reviews ASAP**
   - Ask happy customers immediately after service
   - Send a text with direct review link
   - Offer small incentive (but don't buy reviews - against policy)

2. **Respond to ALL reviews** within 24 hours
   - Thank positive reviewers
   - Address negative reviews professionally
   - Shows you care and boosts ranking

3. **Get consistent reviews** (aim for 2-3 per week)
   - Set up automated post-service review requests
   - Include review link in confirmation emails

### How to Get Your Review Link:
1. Go to your Google Business dashboard
2. Click "Get more reviews"
3. Copy your unique review link
4. Share it via text/email: "Thanks for choosing Boro Appliance Pros! We'd love your feedback: [LINK]"

### NAP Consistency Rules:
**CRITICAL:** Your Name, Address, Phone must be IDENTICAL everywhere.

**Correct:**
- Boro Appliance Pros
- (615) 379-9576
- Murfreesboro, TN 37129

**Wrong (Don't do this):**
- Boro Appliance Pro (missing 's')
- 615-379-9576 (different format)
- Murfreesboro TN (missing comma)

Google uses NAP matching to verify legitimacy. Inconsistencies hurt your ranking.
